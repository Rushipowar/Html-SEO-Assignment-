SEO:
   SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization which is set strategies practieces aimed at improving 
   a website visibility in search engine results pages(SERPs).The primary goal of seo is increase organic
   traffic to website.

Importance:
   - Increased Visibility
   - More Organic traffic
   - Crediblity and Trust
   - Better User Experience
   - Higher Conversion Rates
   - Competitive Advantages
   - Adaption to Changing Trends
